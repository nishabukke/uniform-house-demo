About Us

We introduce ourselves as one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of all kind of uniforms. We pride ourselves on bringing the best range of Corporate Uniforms, Petrol and Gas agency Uniforms, Hotels and Hospital Uniforms, Promotional and Team wear Uniforms Etc. The quality of product is excellent as only fabrics of reputed mills are put to use. Our customers include some of the most reputed companies in the manufacturing sector, petrol and gas agencies company dealing in automated parts and their dealers, hospitals and a large number of hotels and caterers and the product manufacturing companies

We have been the pioneers in starting the readymade era in the Uniform Industry. We maintain a stock of minimum 1000 Sets of each kind of company Uniform. We have been supplying to the petroleum companies since 2001.

We had been awarded with tenders from HPCL, BPCL as well as IOCL for many years. We believe Uniforms are a necessity and necessities should always be stocked up. With this vision we commit to provide delivery to our customers in Maharashtra in maximum 3 working days. With the industry experience and our team of experts we have designed uniforms, which can be altered at the users end and as per the user demand and needs which are provided by them to us.

We have a production unit in Solapur and sales and dispatch center in Aurangabad. Other branches are at Pune and Mumbai, soon we are starting our distribution centers at Akola, Nagpur, Kolhapur and Latur.
